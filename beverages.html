Beverages

Common beverages made with coffee.

Common Beverages in the US

Espresso Shot

Only espresso, no added milk or water.

Latte

Usually made with two shots of espresso plus steamed milk and water. Contains more milk than a cappuccino.

Cappuccino

Made with shots of espresso plus steamed milk and water. Contains less milk and more froth than a latte.

Cafe Americano

Espresso shots with hot water. Notice the thin frothy layer at the surface of the beverage. This is called "crema" - the essence of an espresso made beverage.

Brewed Coffee

Made by pouring hot water to non-espresso coffee beans. Notice no "crema" at the top.

Iced Coffee

Brewed coffee with ice.

Iced Latte

Latte with ice.

Macchiato

Espresso shot with a touch of whipped cream.

Mocha Latte

Latte sweetened with chocolate syrup.
